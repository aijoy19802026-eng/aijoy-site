Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026 — applies to the "Trend Scout Publisher" application

What this application is

Trend Scout Publisher is a personal automation tool operated by a single individual (the "Operator") to publish pre-approved text, image, and video content to social media accounts owned by the Operator, under the AI Joy brand. It is not offered as a service to the public and does not manage accounts on behalf of any third party.

Information we access

The application accesses only what is required to publish content to the Operator's own connected accounts:

  • Account and page identifiers (for example, a TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, or LinkedIn account ID) needed to address the correct destination for a post.
  • Access tokens issued directly by each platform's own authorization flow, used solely to publish content to that same account.

No information is collected from viewers, followers, or any third party who encounters the published content.

How information is stored

Access tokens are stored in an encrypted credential store on infrastructure operated by the Operator and are never written to plaintext files or shared databases. Non-secret routing data (account IDs, category labels) is kept separately in a private database used only to route content to the correct account.

How information is used

Stored credentials are used exclusively to publish content that the Operator has personally reviewed and approved before it is sent to a platform's API. Nothing is used for advertising, profiling, or analytics beyond basic publish-success confirmation.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties. Content is sent only to the platform it is intended for (for example, a TikTok post is sent only to TikTok's API).

Data retention and deletion

Access tokens are retained only for as long as the corresponding account remains connected to the application. The Operator can revoke access at any time via the connected platform's own account settings, which immediately invalidates the stored token.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application. The effective date above indicates the last revision.
