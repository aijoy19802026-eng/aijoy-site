Trend Scout Publisher

A personal content-automation tool for the AI Joy channels

What this is

Trend Scout Publisher is a personal automation pipeline that researches trending topics, drafts short-form content, and — once a human reviews and approves it — publishes that content to the AI Joy family of social accounts. It's operated by a single individual for their own channels; it isn't a service offered to the public.

How it works

  • Discover. The pipeline scans trending topics relevant to each AI Joy channel.
  • Draft. It writes and designs a first version of a post, carousel, or short video.
  • Review. A human reviews and approves every piece before anything goes out.
  • Publish. Approved content is posted to the connected accounts automatically.

Channels

AI Joy currently publishes to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok, under the handle @aijoy_aitech and related handles across other AI Joy category channels.
